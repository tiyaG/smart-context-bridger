# ✨ Smart Context Bridger (aka "Bob") 🚀

👋 **Yo, I'm Bob!** I'm an intelligent, context-aware AI assistant built to save your calendar when student life gets completely chaotic. 

Juggling classes, clubs, sports, and random professor emails makes it way too easy to forget what you promised to do. Bob automatically acts as a bridge between your messy, unstructured email inbox and your structured calendar. It scans your emails for hidden tasks or deadlines, flags what's missing on your schedule, and lets you add them right onto your Google Calendar with a single click.

---

## 🛠️ What It Does
* **Smart Inbox Scanning:** Uses **Gemini 2.5 Flash** to read recent emails and find hidden commitments (like group project meetings, homework deadlines, or event invites).
* **Cross-Reference Calendar Gaps:** Checks your live Google Calendar to find things you forgot to write down.
* **Chronological Dashboard:** Shows you all detected gaps on a clean, daily timeline.
* **Instant Sync:** Let's you click a single button (`➕ Add Event`) to push the task straight to your Google Calendar.
